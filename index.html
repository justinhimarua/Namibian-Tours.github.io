Land of the brave

The Namib Desert along the Atlantic Ocean shore distinguishes Namibia, a nation in southwestern Africa. The nation has a variety of fauna and a considerable population of cheetah.

What to do in Namibia

One of the most surprising things on my African safari was the incredible variety of birds that can be seen.

After a long day of visiting the vast wildlife come back to a rustic camp surrounding yourself with the wild or book a room further away from the wildlife with electricity of course.

You will see baboons all over the safari trail, including sitting on the picnic tables at the rest stops. Some will even jump on the hood of your land cruise mobile. But, finding a mother cradling and grooming her baby is special. Very special.
